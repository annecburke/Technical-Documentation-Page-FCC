What Are Widgets?

Widgets are the vessels through which humanity shapes, interacts, and perceives all the world around them. Widgets are, by their very nature, useful. They are used many times a day by all types of people. They effect changes in the environment around them and to themselves, at times.

There is a wide variety of things of which widgets are capable. Widgets have shape, color, mass, smell, inputs, and outputs. Widgets can be combined with other widgets to enhance their many uses.
